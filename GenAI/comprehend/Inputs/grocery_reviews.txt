The produce section is always fresh, especially the organic apples and spinach.
Customer service is friendly, but the checkout lines tend to get long in the evenings.
I appreciate the variety of international foods available, especially Asian sauces and spices.
Prices are reasonable, though some dairy items are more expensive than other stores.
The bakery section smells amazing and the sourdough bread is worth the trip.
The store layout is clean and easy to navigate; aisles are well-labeled.
I once bought expired yogurt, but the manager quickly apologized and replaced it.
FreshChoice Market’s loyalty program offers good discounts on weekly essentials.
The meat section is well-stocked, but I wish they had more seafood options.
Parking can be tough during weekends, but overall this store is my go-to for groceries.
